I am a home based, certified lash technician!

I specialize in natural looking extensions in order to enhance your natural features, while not making your lashes the focal point of your face. My first set that I asked for was a classic set. Seeing myself with longer lashes helped me to embrace my femininity, and to feel overall more beautiful and confident. It is my goal to give my clients the gift of being able to wake up each day feeling beautiful in their own skin. Everyone is beautiful without extenions, but I have personally experienced the overall confidence booster that lash extensions have given me.

I started my lash extension journey in 2022. I have faced many challenges, but I have been able to overcome them with a great deal of patience, as well as feeling a genuine love for my work. I am so grateful for all of my amazing clients who have been supportive of me since I started. I love being able to connect with each and every one of my clients and learn what set makes them feel the most beautiful.
